This is a working capital financial solution in form of short-term funding given to companies or business owners that need to pay their suppliers for the goods they need to purchase or make an order for their clients or customers.

How it works

This is a revolving loan facility where you are allowed to make quarterly drawings and respective repayments to meet your working capital needs for financing orders. The facility is subject to annual review and renewal subject to performance. The facility comes with competitive interest rate pricing

Benefits of purchase order finance to companies and business owners;

  • Enables the borrower to handle bigger orders beyond their internal working capital.
  • Enhance customer and supplier relationships.
  • Improve their order volume.
  • More profitability for the borrower.
